# PerFiApp

**A Canada-first, bilingual (EN/FR) personal-finance operating system ("FinOS").**

PerFiApp unifies the dozens of money tools people juggle today — rewards trackers,
credit coaches, subscription cancellers, deal finders, budgeting apps — into a single
**financial operating system**. Instead of point solutions that each see only a slice of
your money, PerFiApp aggregates everything, translates it into **CAD + time-to-goal
context**, and ties every recommendation back to your real budget, cash-flow, and credit
state.

## Why PerFiApp

Most tools optimize one thing in isolation:

- **CardPointers** tells you the best card for points — but ignores your budget and utilization.
- **Honey** finds the best coupon — but doesn't know if you should buy at all.
- **Rocket Money / Mint** surface subscriptions and bills — but don't orchestrate pay order or cash safety.
- **Cleo / Borrowell** coach credit — but separately from your rewards and spending.

PerFiApp's edge is **integration**: every suggestion ("use this card", "wait to buy this",
"cancel that subscription", "pay this bill early") is evaluated against your actual
financial picture and expressed in dollars and time-to-goal — bilingual, and built for
Canadian programs, banks, and rules first.

## Modules

PerFiApp is organized into module tabs, each with focused submodules:

| Module | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Rewards & Loyalty** | Points wallet, Canada-first perks DB, best-card recommender, offer auto-activation, welcome-bonus and statement-credit tracking, deal radar, status tracking. |
| **Credit & Coaching** | Credit monitoring, due-date & utilization coaching, credit-builder playbooks, refinance & card-lineup optimization. |
| **Cash Safety & Autopilot** | Forward-looking runway with low-balance/fee guard, transparent cash-advance bridge, rules-based sweeps & roundups. |
| **Bills & Subscriptions** | Subscription radar, one-tap cancellation/negotiation, free-trial guard, bill calendar with safe-to-pay dates. |
| **Pay & Payment Optimization** | Best card/account at checkout, payment sequencer to avoid overdrafts and maximize progress. |
| **Shopping & Deals** | Auto-coupons, price watch & droplist, buy-now-vs-wait score tied to budget and goals. |
| **Tasks & To-Dos** | Money-aware tasks linked to bills/merchants/goals, smart scheduling around paydays. |
| **Habits & Routines** | Gamified habits and cross-module daily rituals tied to real progress. |
| **Focus & Mental Health** | Money-stress packs and wind-down sequences that turn worries into concrete actions. |
| **Inbox & Notifications** | Email subscription clean-up and a single actionable FR/EN money digest. |
| **Travel & Trips** | Itinerary builder aware of travel budget, FX, and insurance; travel stats and spend. |
| **Life Admin & Docs** | Receipt & warranty vault, important-document wallet with reminders. |
| **Workspace & Playbooks** | Life-event playbooks and a finance notebook that auto-references live FinOS data. |
| **Household & Family** | Family dashboard with roles/permissions, kid money & allowances. |
| **Social & Accountability** | Accountability circles tied to real budget and goal progress. |

## Differentiators

- **Aggregate everything** across Canadian and global programs in one place.
- **Translate to CAD + time-to-goal** so every number is meaningful.
- **Decisions grounded in real state** — budget, cash flow, credit, and goals — not points alone.
- **Canada-first and bilingual (EN/FR)** by design.
