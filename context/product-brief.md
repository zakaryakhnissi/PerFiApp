# FinOS Product Brief

## Vision

**PerFiApp** is a Canada-first, bilingual (EN/FR) personal-finance operating system ("FinOS"). It unifies the dozens of money tools people juggle — rewards trackers, credit coaches, subscription cancellers, deal finders, budgeting apps — into a single platform where every recommendation is grounded in the user's real budget, cash-flow, and credit state.

## The Core Problem

Today's tools optimize one dimension in isolation:

| Tool | What it does well | What it ignores |
|---|---|---|
| CardPointers | Best card for points | Budget, utilization |
| Honey | Best coupon at checkout | Whether you should buy at all |
| Rocket Money / Mint | Surfaces subscriptions & bills | Payment sequencing, cash safety |
| Cleo / Borrowell | Credit coaching | Rewards, spending context |
| TripIt | Travel itineraries | Travel budget, FX, insurance |

FinOS's edge: **every suggestion is evaluated against the full financial picture**, expressed in CAD and time-to-goal, bilingual, and built for Canadian programs and rules first.

## Key Differentiators

1. **Aggregate everything** across Canadian and global loyalty programs in one place.
2. **CAD + time-to-goal** translation on every number — no raw points or abstract scores.
3. **Decisions grounded in real state** — budget, cash flow, credit, and goals evaluated together.
4. **Canada-first and bilingual (EN/FR)** by design, not as an add-on.
5. **Integration intelligence** — every module feeds every other (e.g. best-card recommendation checks budget AND utilization AND rewards simultaneously).
